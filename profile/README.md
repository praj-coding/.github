## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction
**Impact**: Inappropriate language or behavior deemed unprofessional.  
**Consequence**: Private written warning, with clarity around the violation. Public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning
**Impact**: A violation through a single incident or series of actions.  
**Consequence**: Formal warning with restrictions on interaction for a set period.

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Impact**: Serious violation of community standards or sustained inappropriate behavior.  
**Consequence**: Temporary ban from interaction or communication with the community.

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Impact**: Pattern of violations, harassment, or aggression toward individuals/groups.  
**Consequence**: Permanent ban from public interaction within the community.
